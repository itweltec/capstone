Project Synopsis

The Sadhana Card is a mobile application developed to help the devotees of Lord Krishna connected under the ISCKON Culture for Education. It helps devotees to track their devotional practices and participation in devotional events by converting that into points, based on the time and activity entered by devotee which gives a complete progress report on monthly, quarterly, annually basis.
